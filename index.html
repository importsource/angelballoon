Angel’s Balloon
Spreading joy, one balloon at a time — for over ten years.

Every day, in the heart of the park, a gentle grandpa arrives with a bag full of colorful balloons. For over ten years, he has greeted children with a warm smile, offering them free balloons and a moment of pure joy. His hands, though wrinkled with age, move with the grace of love as he blows up each balloon, tying them with care and handing them to eager little hands.

According to nearby residents, this grandpa has been coming here for over a decade—never missing a day—just to hand out balloons to children for free.

Rain or shine, his presence is a beacon of kindness. The laughter of children, the sparkle in their eyes, and the chorus of thank-yous are his daily reward. His unwavering compassion has touched countless families, reminding us all of the power of simple, selfless acts.

Balloon of Angel is a charitable organization inspired by his story. We celebrate his legacy and invite you to join us in spreading joy, one balloon at a time.
